My Values

When I think of the times of my life where I've been the happiest, proudest, or most satisfied, the following values come to mind, and on a scale of 1 to 5 on how much I try to live up to these values:

  • Accomplishment - 5
  • Amibition - 5
  • Autonomy - 5
  • Challenge - 4
  • Control - 4
  • Freedom - 4
  • Friendships - 4
  • Growth - 4
  • Honesty - 4
  • Hospitality - 2
  • Insight - 5
  • Introspection - 5
  • Knowledge - 5
  • Leadership - 2
  • Responsibility - 4

One value in particular that is important to me is being challenged. I like to learn in general, and the best way to do so is through adversity/challenge. Challenges motivate me to do my best, give me an opportunity to show what I am (or am not) capable of, and often teach me something either educational or something about myself. Challenges test the extent of my abilities and give me something to proud of after completing.
